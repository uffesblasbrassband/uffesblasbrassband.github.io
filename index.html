Uffes Blås Brassband

Uffes Blås Brassband startades 2007. Bandet var ursprungligen ett samarbete mellan brasslärare på ett antal kulturskolor i västsverige och syftet var att ge äldre elever på gymnasienivå möjlighet att utvecklas och musicera tillsammans med likasinnade på en högre nivå.

Med tiden anslöt sig fler lärare tillsammans med sina elever och idag består bandet av en blandning av lärare, elever och duktiga amatörer (varav flera naturligtvis är tidigare elever). Bandet har ingen fast geografisk bas men träffas oftast för repetitioner i Uddevalla eller Skövde. De flesta medlemmarna kommer från olika orter i mellansverige.

Bandet har deltagit i tävlingarna vid Svenska Brassbandfestivalen sedan 2007 då man ställde upp i dåvarande b-klassen. Bandet har sedan klättrat från division 2 till elitdivisionen med en 4:e plats 2017 som hittills bästa placering.

Dirigent

Andreas Kratz was born in Tranås 1979. He is raised within the Salvation Army and started playing in the Tranås Band of the Salvation Army at an early age under his father Torbjörn. 1997-2000, Andreas studied trombone at the Academy of Music and Drama, University of Gothenburg, where he studied with Professor Ingemar Roos. Between 2003-2017 Andreas works as a Tenor & Bass trombonist at the Gothenburg Opera House. Today he work at Head of music in Malung - Salen and is an active freelance conductor.

Andreas has always been interested in the art of conducting and he has undertook conducting studies at Gothenburg Universty of Music and with Professor David King & Garry Cutt. In the past years he has frequently been invited as guest conductor with several brass bands and wind bands in Europe!

He have worked with solist and conductors like Prof. Garry Cutt, Prof. David King, Phillip McCann, David Childs, James Morrison, James Fountain, Lucy Cutt, Tom Davoren, Prof. Ingemar Roos, Richard Phillips, Glenn van Looy, Lucy Cutt, Richard Marshall, Owen Farr, Michael Becquet, Chris Houlding, Mogens Andresen, Nils Landgren, Stefan Schulz, Øystein Baadsvik.

Andreas was the Co-founder, Chairman and Resident Conductor of Windcorp Brassband 2005-2016. He has won the Swedish National Championships as a conductor and player on 11 occasions and represented Sweden at the European Championships 5 times.

In July 2009 Andreas was invited to play Solo Trombone with Fodens Band at The English Nationals in Preston and with Grimethorpe Colliery Band at British Open 2011.

In 2012 he made a short apperance with The Fairey Band on 2nd euphonium at the Whit Friday Marches Contest.

In 2015 he was visiting trombone tutor at National Youth Brassband of Wales.

In 2016 Andreas conducted Windcorp Brassband at Whit Friday Marches to a 5th place in Delph and 1st place at Uppermill. This was the first time a non UK Band won that contest.

Andreas was a a member of Wallberg Brass Band (Schweiz) 2011-2015.

In february 2018 Andreas was appointed Professional Conductor for Tongwynlais Temperance Band, Wales. He made his first appearance with the band at the Welsh Area contest. After qualifying he is now the first swedish conductor ever to conduct at the National Final Championships of Great Britain in Royal Albert Hall, London october 2018.
